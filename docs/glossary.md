# Glossary

Plain-language definitions for the words you'll bump into.

**Anchor** — A regulated service that swaps crypto for regular money and sends it
to your bank. Olio uses one for cash-out; you give it your bank details directly.

**Blockchain** — A public, shared record of transactions. Great for trust, bad for
privacy by default — which is the gap Olio fills.

**CCTP** — Circle's official way to move USDC between chains (Ethereum, Solana,
etc.) and into Olio on Stellar. Real USDC, not a wrapped IOU.

**Claimable balance** — A Stellar feature that lets Olio pay out to a wallet even
if it isn't set up to hold USDC yet.

**Disclosure** — Voluntarily proving one specific payment happened (for taxes,
accounting, your bank) without revealing your other payments.

**Gas** — The small network fee blockchains charge per action. Olio covers it for
you.

**Nullifier** — A one-time "used" stamp that stops a payment from being spent
twice — without revealing which deposit it came from.

**Note** — A private record of a payment inside the pool. Only you can see and
spend your notes.

**Passkey** — Your device's fingerprint/face unlock, used to sign in and approve
transactions. No seed phrase required.

**PIN** — Your 6-digit backup and recovery key. Keep it safe; Olio will never ask
for it.

**Self-custody** — You hold the keys; Olio can't spend, freeze, or lose your
money. The trade-off is that recovery depends on you.

**Shielded pool** — The shared "vault" where payments live as private notes,
mixed together so nobody can link them to you.

**Stablecoin** — Crypto designed to hold a steady value. USDC is one, worth ~$1.

**Stellar** — The fast, low-cost blockchain Olio runs on.

**Unlinkability** — Olio's core promise: nobody can connect the money coming in to
the money you take out.

**USDC** — A digital US dollar. What you get paid in.

**Username** — Your friendly handle (like `@dinar`) that people pay instead of a
scary wallet address.

**Viewing key** — The key that lets you (and only you) discover which payments in
the pool are yours.

**Wallet / address** — Your crypto account and its public "account number." Olio
hides yours behind a username and keeps incoming payments private.

**Zero-knowledge proof** — Math that lets you prove something is true (like "I own
this payment") without revealing the details behind it.
